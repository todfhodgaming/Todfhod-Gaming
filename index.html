Event Management

Events, being the core of esports, is our prime feature. We host online and offline events globally, working with top esports game studios and publishers. ACBC Esports is famous for its trail-blazing events, high quality broadcasting, flawless management and exemplary communication. We have always ensured a hundred percent transparency when it comes to prize money distribution, production and management.

ACBC Esports has successfully pulled off some of the largest-scale Esports events in India. Till date, we have organized events like MAHAYUDH, ZERO HOUR SHOWDOWN, MIDNIGHT, Battle of the Dead, IESL Sponser league, some of the most anticipated Esports events across all the gaming platforms.

We have expanded our horizons to a global level already, organizing and running the ZERO HOUR SHOWDOWN –2 PUBGM | India. We plan to have a jam-packed calendar full of Esports madness in the upcoming days.
